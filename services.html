Common Questions

How long is each session?

Each individual therapy session typically lasts for 50-60 minutes. Couple or family sessions may extend up to 90 minutes depending on the requirement.

Is my information confidential?

Absolutely. We adhere to strict ethical guidelines regarding client confidentiality. Your personal information and session details are secure.

Do you offer online sessions?

Yes, we specialize in online mental wellness. You can connect with us via secure video or audio calls from the comfort of your home.

How do I know if I need therapy?

If you're feeling overwhelmed, persistently sad, anxious, or unable to cope with daily life, therapy can be beneficial. You don't need a diagnosable condition to seek support.
